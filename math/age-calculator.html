📖 About Age Calculation

This calculator computes your exact chronological age — the number of complete years, months, and days since your date of birth. This is the standard age used in legal, medical, and official contexts.

The date difference mode is useful for: counting down to an event, calculating the length of a project, finding how many days since a milestone, or checking how long you've been together with someone.
