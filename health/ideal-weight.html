📖 How Ideal Weight Is Calculated

Different medical formulas estimate ideal body weight (IBW). The Devine formula is the most widely used in clinical settings. Results vary slightly between formulas.

FormulaMaleFemale
Devine (1974)50 + 2.3 × (ht − 60) kg45.5 + 2.3 × (ht − 60) kg
Robinson (1983)52 + 1.9 × (ht − 60) kg49 + 1.7 × (ht − 60) kg
Miller (1983)56.2 + 1.41 × (ht − 60) kg53.1 + 1.36 × (ht − 60) kg
Hamwi (1964)48 + 2.7 × (ht − 60) kg45.5 + 2.2 × (ht − 60) kg

ht = height in inches. These formulas were developed for adults and may not apply to children or athletes.
