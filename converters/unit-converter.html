📏 All-in-One Unit Converter

Convert between metric, imperial, and other units across 6 categories: length, weight, temperature, area, volume, and speed. Instant results as you type.

⇅

📖 Common Conversions Reference

📏 Length

  • 1 inch = 2.54 cm  |  1 foot = 30.48 cm = 12 inches
  • 1 yard = 0.9144 m  |  1 mile = 1.609 km
  • 1 meter = 3.281 feet  |  1 km = 0.6214 miles

⚖️ Weight

  • 1 pound (lb) = 0.4536 kg  |  1 ounce = 28.35 g
  • 1 kg = 2.205 lbs  |  1 stone = 14 lbs = 6.35 kg
  • 1 ton (US) = 2,000 lbs = 907.2 kg

🌡️ Temperature

°F = °C × 9/5 + 32
K = °C + 273.15
  • Water freezes: 0°C = 32°F = 273.15 K
  • Water boils: 100°C = 212°F = 373.15 K
  • Body temperature: 37°C = 98.6°F

📐 Area

  • 1 acre = 43,560 sq ft = 4,047 m²
  • 1 hectare = 10,000 m² = 2.471 acres
  • 1 sq mile = 640 acres = 2.59 km²

🧪 Volume

  • 1 US gallon = 3.785 liters  |  1 liter = 0.264 gallons
  • 1 cup = 8 fl oz = 237 mL
  • 1 cubic meter = 1,000 liters

🚀 Speed

  • 1 mph = 1.609 km/h  |  1 km/h = 0.621 mph
  • 1 m/s = 3.6 km/h = 2.237 mph
  • 1 knot = 1.852 km/h = 1.151 mph
